A Family Tradition of Excellence

Welcome to PRIME GRILL 1302, where family tradition meets culinary excellence in the heart of Condado, San Juan. Our story is one of passion, dedication, and a commitment to serving the finest prime hand-cut meats in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

We believe in the power of gathering around a table, sharing exceptional food, and creating lasting memories. Every cut of meat is carefully selected, every dish thoughtfully prepared, and every guest treated like family.

From our farm-to-table approach to our dedication to sourcing the best local ingredients from Puerto Rico, we honor both tradition and innovation in every plate we serve.
